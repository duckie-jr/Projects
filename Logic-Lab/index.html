Logic Lab

Build gates, memory, clocks & whole computers.

Higher = slower. A NOT → Delay loop blinks at this rate.

How to use

  • Click a part above to drop it on the board.
  • Drag a part to move it.
  • Drag from one pin to another to wire them.
  • Click a Switch to flip 0/1.
  • Hold a Button for a quick pulse.
  • Double-click a Delay to set its tick length.
  • Build your own clock: loop a NOT back through a Delay.
  • Select a part or wire, press Delete to remove.
  • Scroll to zoom, drag the background to pan.
